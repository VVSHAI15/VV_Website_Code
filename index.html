AviShai's Story

AviShai Dayanim is a composer, orchestrator, producer, and storyteller based in the Washington, D.C. and Baltimore, M.D. area. He is currently a senior Dual Degree student pursuing both a Bachelor of Music in Music for New Media and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the Peabody Institute and the Johns Hopkins University.

From an early age, AviShai has been captivated by the power of music to tell stories and evoke deep emotions. He has composed scores for short films, theater productions, and video games — earning multiple awards at Film Festivals, most recently for his work on The Velvet Rose. His love for storytelling through music also drives his innovative sound design, crafting unique virtual instruments that bring new dimensions to his compositions.

In May 2023, AviShai embarked on an exciting venture into virtual instrument creation through an internship at Impact Soundworks LLC. He has since had the honor of working alongside renowned composers including Michael Levine (Megamind, The Simpsons Movie), Mac Quayle (Mr. Robot, American Horror Story), and Henry Jackman (Captain America, X-Men, Kingsman).
