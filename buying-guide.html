Virtual Library Buying Guide

IN PROGRESS

Native Instrument's Komplete Start

This powerful free bundle from Native Instruments is one of the best starting off points for virtual libraries. Containing a wide array of sounds to cover all your needs, as well as Kontakt Player, which will serve as the host for most of the other free libraries.

Impact Soundworks Freebies

A fantastic and high quality selection featuring a guitar, bass, synths, and percussion. Most importantly, Palette: Primary Colors, an orchestral library, can serve as an excellent starting point for composing orchestral music.

Heavyocity Foundations

An epic collection of sounds to write impactful and cinematic score. With everything from strings, to brass and choirs, this is the perfect tool to turn your music up a notch.

Vital Wavetable Synth

My go to free synth - based on Serum and designed to work just as well as a professional paid product. If you learn how to use this synth, it can do anything you need it to.

Project Sam Free Orchestras

Another collection of epic and cinematic sounds while also introducing cool multi-instrument options. A go to choice for creating cinematic soundscapes and action sequences.

Spitfire Audio BBCSO Discover Orchestra

A fantastic full orchestra that can serve all of your needs when starting off. It uses the same samples as BBCSO Pro, an incredible orchestral library, simply with less options. Paired with Palette: Primary Colors, this library can cover your bases for orchestral music.

Crow on the Hill Vaults

This ever-growing collection of unique sounds presents emotional and inspiring instruments, desgined to power the creative process of composition and to write incredible music.

Spitfire Audio's Original Series - $29

If you want to elevate your music beyond free libraries, this is the place to look. The Spitfire Original Series is some of the highest quality "bang for your buck" that you can find. Their strings, pianos and percussion in particular can easily be used in a professional setting.

Native Instruments Komplete Select - $99

An upgrade from the free Native Instruments Bundle that includes an incredible upright piano, powerful synths like Massive, and other cinematic libraries such as Ethereal Earth and Hybrid Keys.

Audio Imperia Nucleus Lite - $99

If you find yourself writing a lot of epic orchestral music and needing an upgrade from the free content, Nucleus Lite is the perfect choice. A smaller version of Audio Imperia's flagship Nucleus library, Nucleus lite allows you to elevate your orchestral music composition for cheap.

Spitfire Audio Albion One - $499

Ablion One is the flagship All-In-One library. Part of Spitfire Audio's Alblion Series, each tailored to a specific genre, Albion One leans towards the cinematic and epic music route. With everything from orchestral ensemble patches, to an incredible collection of percussion, to complex and powerful synths and loops, Ablion One truly has everything you need. If epic music isn't your thing, libraries like Ablion Neo and Tundra serve as great choices for more contemporary and dramatic music while Albion Solstice is tailored to intimate and folky scores.

Orchestral Tools Metropolis Ark 1 - $550

Metropolis Ark is the flagship All-In-One series for Orchestral Tools. With each library in the series building off of the other, Metropolis Ark 1 serves as the perfect base for cinematic music, offering an incredible orchestral palette, choir and other sounds, while also using Orchestral Tool's Sine player.

Audio Imperia Jaeger - $599

The big angry sister to Audio Imperia's Nucleus, Jaeger is a massive sounding library and one of the most encompassing All-In-One libraries. With an entire orchestra with dozens of articulations, including legato, as well as percussion, vocals and sound design, and even having both instrument and ensemble patches, this library truly checks off all the boxes for the modern cinematic All-In-One library.

Spitfire Audio BBCSO Core- $499

The upgrade to Spitfire's Discover Orchestra, BBCSO Core is the golden standard concert library. With incredible legatos, every articulation that you will ever need, and all the instruments and sounds of a concert orchestra - this is a great starting point for an orchestral composer.
