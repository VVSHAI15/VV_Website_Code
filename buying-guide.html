Virtual Library Buying Guide

Virtual Libraries are some of the most important tools when it comes to being a film composer. Libraries give us the ability to create powerful mockups and to capture our vision as composers inside of a DAW. The world of plugins is saturated, expensive, and sometimes, a bit dishonest. More expensive libraries do not translate to a better composition, and a skilled composer can write incredible music with budget libraries.
